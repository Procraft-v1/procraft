Resume Builder

Build an online resume and create your public profile

A resume quickly explains who you are to employers. Procraft connects your resume data with your portfolio to present it as a professional public profile.

What makes a great resume?

A great resume is short, clear and results-oriented. It should display work experience, education, skills, projects and contact information in a clean, readable format.

How does Procraft help you build a resume?

  • Stores all profile data in one dashboard.
  • Organizes experience, projects, education and skill blocks.
  • Shows resume data in greater depth via a portfolio link.
  • Provides a ready structure for PDF resume export.

Why include a portfolio link in your resume?

Resumes are often brief. A portfolio link lets employers see your projects, certificates and real experience in more detail. That's why an online resume paired with a portfolio produces stronger results.

Who can use it?

Professionals looking for work, students, junior developers, designers, freelancers and anyone who wants to present themselves professionally can use Procraft to build a resume.
